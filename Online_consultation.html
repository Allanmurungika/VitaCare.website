Online Consultation Services

What is Online Consultation?

Online consultation offers a secure, convenient way to consult with our healthcare professionals from the comfort of your home. Whether you have questions about your health, need advice on treatment, or require follow-up care, we're here to help.

Services Offered

  • General Consultations for Health Concerns
  • Follow-Up Consultations on Ongoing Treatments
  • Medication Management and Refills
  • Virtual Mental Health Counseling
  • Nutrition and Lifestyle Coaching
  • Specialist Referrals Through Telemedicine
  • Access to Lab Results and Medical Records

How to Access Online Consultation Services

Accessing our online consultation services is easy:

  1. Schedule an Appointment: Use our online booking system to choose a date and time that fits your schedule.
  2. Prepare for Your Consultation: Have any relevant medical information ready for your appointment.
  3. Join the Consultation: Log in to your secure telehealth portal at the scheduled time.
  4. Follow-Up Care: Receive a care plan, prescriptions, or referrals as needed after your consultation.
