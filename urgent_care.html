Urgent Care Services

Your immediate solution for non-life-threatening conditions.

What is Urgent Care?

Urgent care provides prompt treatment for acute illnesses and injuries that require immediate attention but are not severe enough to warrant a visit to the emergency room. Our skilled team is available to ensure you receive the care you need when you need it most.

Services Offered

  • Minor Injury Treatment (e.g., sprains, fractures)
  • Illness Diagnosis (e.g., colds, flu, infections)
  • Wound Care and Stitching
  • Diagnostic Imaging (X-rays, etc.)
  • Laboratory Testing (blood tests, urinalysis)
  • Vaccinations and Preventive Care
  • Work and School Physicals
  • Referral Services to Specialists

How to Access Urgent Care Services

Accessing our urgent care services is quick and easy:

  1. Walk-In or Schedule an Appointment: Visit us in-person or book through our online system.
  2. Registration: Complete your registration quickly to minimize wait times.
  3. Consult with Our Provider: Receive a thorough evaluation and treatment plan tailored to your needs.
  4. Follow-Up Care: Our team will provide guidance on any necessary follow-up steps or referrals.
